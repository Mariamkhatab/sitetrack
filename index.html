🐛
Full Issue Logging

Log every snag with title, description, date, and precise location on site. Capture everything before it falls through the cracks.

🎯
Priority Management

Flag issues as High, Medium, or Low priority. Instantly filter your view to focus on what matters most and escalate critical defects fast.

👷
Party Assignment

Assign each issue to the responsible party — Subcontractor A, MEP Team, Civil Team, and more. Full accountability, built in.

📊
Status Tracking

Move issues through Open → In Progress → Closed. At a glance, see the live closure rate for your site or handover package.

📁
Local JSON Storage

All data is saved locally as JSON files — no internet, no cloud, no accounts. Your snagging data stays on your machine.

⚡
Instant Excel Export

One-click export to a multi-sheet Excel report. Perfect for client handovers, weekly QA reviews, and contractor accountability meetings.
